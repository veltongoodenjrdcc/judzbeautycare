Questions & Answers

The Questions We Hear Most Often.

Quick answers to what clients usually ask. If yours is not here, just message us and we will reply promptly.

How do I book an appointment?

The simplest way is to send us a WhatsApp message. Tap any WhatsApp button on this page and we will reply to confirm your day and time. We are open Monday to Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM.

How much does a gel nail set cost?

Our standard gel sets are J$3,000. We are currently running a launch promotion for the month of May: the first 10 clients pay J$2,000, and the next 20 pay J$2,500. Message us to find out how many promo spots are still available.

How long does a gel nail appointment take?

A full gel set takes approximately one hour. More intricate designs with detailed nail art may take a little longer. We will always give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

Where exactly are you located?

We are at Cherry Gardens Plaza, Kitson Town, St. Catherine, Jamaica. You can find us on the map in the Contact section below. Message us on WhatsApp if you need directions.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Kitson Town, St. Catherine and surrounding communities. If you are coming from further away, just message us. We are happy to accommodate where we can.

What nail styles do you offer?

We do plain gel sets, French tips, chrome finishes, accent nails and custom designs. If you have a reference photo or a specific look in mind, send it to us on WhatsApp and we will let you know if we can match it.

Do you take walk-ins or is it appointment only?

We recommend booking ahead via WhatsApp to secure your slot and avoid waiting. We do our best to accommodate walk-ins when availability allows, but booked clients are always prioritised.

What should I do to prepare before my appointment?

For nails, remove any old polish before you arrive if possible. For pedicures, come with clean feet; we take care of everything else. For make-up, arrive with a clean, moisturised face. If you have any allergies or sensitivities, let us know when you book.

Do you sell nail care products?

Yes. We carry Nail Care Oil for all Nailz in Jojoba Oil and Grapeseed Oil variants, plus Cuticle Oils for Gel Nailz in Aloe Vera, Lavender and Rose. Each is J$500. Ask about availability when you visit or message us.

What makes Judz Beauty Care different from other salons?

We are a locally owned business that genuinely cares about every client. Judith is personally involved in every visit, ensuring you get real attention and not a rushed experience. We combine professional results with prices that everyday clients can actually afford, and we go the extra mile with touches like our hot stone foot massage to make the experience something you look forward to.

Still have a question? Send us a message and we will get back to you.
